About the Gym

Here at Bust a Move Fitness our goal is to bring fun diverse workouts to a everyone. It is uncommon to find such diversity in a rural town of less than 5000. Offering classes such as Pilates, Yoga, Bootcamp, Zumba, Drumsticks +, Cardio Hip hop, low impact classes,and more. Ashley has always been passionate about encouraging others while making it entertaining as well. Established Novemer of 2019 we are a newer gym ready to meet the needs of our clients. Our studio is currently closed due to the COVID-19 non essential business order. We will be opening back up as soon as this is all over. We have set up Youtube videos to continue serving our clients during these trying times.
